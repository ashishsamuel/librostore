Books of the Month

Book Details

Book Name : The Alchemist

Author : Paulo Coelho

The Alchemist is a novel by Brazilian author Paulo Coelho which was first published in 1988. Originally written in Portuguese, it became a widely translated international bestseller. An allegorical novel, The Alchemist follows a young Andalusian shepherd in his journey to the pyramids of Egypt, after having a recurring dream of finding a treasure there.

Book Details

Book Name : The Story of my Experiments with Truth

Author : M.K Gandhi

The Story of My Experiments with Truth ( lit'Experiments of Truth or Autobiography') is the autobiography of Mahatma Gandhi, covering his life from early childhood through to 1921. It was written in weekly installments and published in his journal Navjivan from 1925 to 1929. Its English translation also appeared in installments in his other journal Young India.

Book Details

Book Name : Ignited Minds

Author : A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Unleashing the Power Within India (2002,) is a book written by Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam, President of India from 2002 to 2007. The book is a logical step forward from Dr. Kalam's earlier book, India 2020: A Vision for the New Millennium (1998,) and consists of many inspirational messages by Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam.

Book Details

Book Name : Life is What you make it

Author : Preeti Shenoy

Life Is What You Make It is a novel by Preeti Shenoy. The book was in "Top books of 2011" as per the Nielsen list which is published in Hindustan Times. It was also on Times of India all-time best sellers of 2011. This is an astonishing love story set in India in 1990s. This is a book of love, hope and how determination can overcome even destiny.
